Meet The Team

Our instructors are highly experienced

Lekshmi PS
Lekshmi brings 9 years of expertise in German language training and is certified by the Goethe-Institut Mannheim, Germany & Goethe-Institut , MMB, Calcutta .Her classes combine deep linguistic knowledge with personalized attention, making her a trusted guide for students at all levels.
Sunita Bhatia
Sunita has a vast teaching experience of 38 years, both in Germany and India.She has worked for the Goethe Institute for several years and specializes in teaching higher levels upto C2
Arun Reynold
Arun is a dedicated IELTS trainer with 2 years of experience helping students reach their target band scores. His methodical approach focuses on all four modules, ensuring students build confidence and competence for the exam.
Riju Francis
With 6 years of teaching experience, Riju specializes in guiding learners through the intricacies of the German language. His structured lessons and supportive teaching style have helped many students achieve success in Goethe exams.
Armin Hermann Hurt
A native of Munich in southern Germany, Armin has 3 years of experience teaching German in offline settings. His culturally rich and practical lessons offer students an authentic and engaging language learning experience.
