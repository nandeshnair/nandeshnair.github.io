IELTS

10 Months

This intensive 10-month IELTS course is designed to take learners from beginner (A1) to advanced (C1) proficiency. The program follows a structured curriculum aligned with international standards and includes comprehensive coverage of course books across all levels.

To ensure strong language acquisition, the course includes

Daily vocabulary tests to build and retain essential word banks.
Weekly class tests for continuous assessment.
Module-wise practice sessions to reinforce grammar and skills.
Mock exams and internal assessments for exam readiness.

Whether you’re aiming to study abroad, boost your career, or explore new cultures, Edelweiss is your first step — because Speaking Connects.
